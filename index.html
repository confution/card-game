War Card Game

Instructions for War:

  • Both Players draw a card
  • Players compare cards
  • Player with higher card wins
  • If players have same card:
    • Declare war: each player puts one card face down and another face up
    • Player with higher card wins all 6 (3 from each player)
    • If the result of war is another tie, then process repeats and winner gets all 10 cards (5 from each player)
  • Winner of each hand takes all cards played on that hand
  • The game is over when one player has all cards from deck
